## What to Watch in Logs

### Step-0 Diagnostics
- `per_token_cb: harmful mask active X/Y | benign mask active X/Y` — verify benign mask is NON-ZERO
- `simplified_pooled: harmful pool mask active X/Y | benign pool mask active X/Y` — same

### Every 50 Steps
```
[Step N] mode=per_token_cb | L_b=0.xxxx L_h=0.xxxx L_kl=0.xxxx d_h=0.xxx d_b=0.xxx
```
- **L_kl MUST be non-zero** (was zero before fix). If zero → bug not fixed
- **d_h should increase** (harmful reps pushed away from frozen)
- **d_b should stay small** (benign reps stay close to frozen)
- **grad_norm MUST be non-zero** (dl2rc fix). If zero → distance fix not working

### What "Good" Looks Like
- L_h starts high (~1.6 = margin), decreases as harmful is pushed past margin
- L_b starts at 0, stays near 0 (benign not drifting)
- L_kl stable and non-zero (~0.1-1.0 range)
- d_h grows from ~0 to >margin_h (1.6)
- d_b stays <margin_b (1.2)

## Known Risks / Open Items

1. **Margin calibration**: 1.2/1.6 tuned for cosine [0,2]. With dl2rc (L2+10*cos), scale is different. Phase 2 sweep tests {1.0, 1.6, 2.5}
2. **triplet_full still has cluster center**: Known noisy with batch=4. This is intentional for Phase 1 (isolate bug-fix contribution)
3. **Benign data was being filtered in ALL previous injection_aware sweeps**: Sweep 5's 21% benign correct was with only ~250 benign samples (resisted AD traces). With 16K+ benign samples now, results may change significantly
